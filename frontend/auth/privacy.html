Privacy Policy

PREACT VISION

PREACT VISION is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how information is collected, processed, and used. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data handling and user privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect limited personal information such as usernames, email addresses, and login credentials strictly for authentication and system access purposes. Video feeds and behavioral data may be processed for security and analysis but are not stored unless explicitly required.

2. Use of Information

Collected information is used solely to operate, maintain, and improve the PREACT VISION platform. Behavioral analytics are utilized to detect anomalies, generate alerts, and enhance situational awareness.

3. Data Privacy & Anonymization

PREACT VISION prioritizes privacy-preserving technologies. Facial anonymization, blurring, and non-identifiable representations are applied wherever possible to protect individual identities.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to safeguard information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

5. Third-Party Services

PREACT VISION does not sell, rent, or trade personal data to third parties. External services, if used, comply with strict privacy and security standards.

6. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials and ensuring lawful use of the platform.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect system improvements or regulatory changes. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related concerns or inquiries, please contact the PREACT VISION administration team.
